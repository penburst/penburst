Nada Fashion

®

was established in 2016 in Zurich, Switzerland and has been in operation across Switzerland’s suburbs ever since. We started out as a small time retail store for baby wear and soon graduated into selling women’s and later on men’s clothing. We recently ventured into sportswear, shoes and underwear just to make sure that you have a complete set shopping with us.

The Management

We are upbeat about effective management and we keep a sizeable yet capable management team. Meet our managers.

Location and Distribution Points

We operate on a multi-platform basis whereby we make sure that you can conveniently access our items through: our stall in Zurich, online orders to home deliveries, point of contact sales. You can shop easily with us.

Our Aim

We strive for success in keeping you smart, professional, comfortable and conveniently dressed for whatever occasion that you have to attend.
